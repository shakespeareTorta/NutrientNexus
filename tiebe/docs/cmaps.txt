# Concept Map Transcription

## Central Question (top, gray box)
**"What challenges within either, or both SDGs 2 and 14, can be addressed by autonomous systems, and what role can digital twins play to meet these challenges?"**

---

## Top-Level Concepts

**Global Sustainability Challenges** *(italic label)*
- include →
  - **Zero Hunger (SDG 2)** *(yellow)*
  - **Life Below Water (SDG 14)** *(teal)*

---

## SDG 2 Branch (Left Side)

**Zero Hunger (SDG 2)**
- mandates → **Double Smallholder Production (Target 2.3)**
  - measured by → **Production Volume Per Labour Unit (Indicator 2.3.1)** *(pink)*
    - leads to → **Higher yield and profit** *(bold)*
      - increases → Tax income → benefits → **Government** *(yellow)*
      - leads to → **Lower food prices** → benefits → **Consumers** *(yellow)*

**Life Below Water (SDG 14)**
- mandates → **Sustainable Food Production (Target 2.4)** *(orange)*
  - measured by → **Proportion of Sustainable Agricultural Area (Indicator 2.4.1)** *(pink)*
  - allows for → **Longer land usage** *(bold)*
    - leads to → Reduced demand for new land
      - results in → **Reduced habitat loss** *(bold gray)*
        - benefits → **Forests and natural habitats** *(yellow)*
    - results in → Reduced spending for developing new land

**Life Below Water (SDG 14)**
- mandates → **Reduce Marine Pollution (Target 14.1)** *(orange)*
  - measured by → **Coastal Eutrophication Index (Indicator 14.1.1a)** *(pink)*
    - decreased by → Overuse of irrigation and fertilisers

---

## Central Problem Chain

**Farmers** *(yellow)*
- use → **Fertilisers**
  - cause → **Agricultural nutrient runoff** *(bold)*
    - causes → **Algae blooms** *(bold)*
      - cause → Less light/oxygen in water
        - causes → Less plant growth → harms → **Ocean life** *(yellow)*
      - cause → Toxins release
        - causes → **Harm to humans** *(bold)*
          - leads to → Water poisoning
          - increases → **Long-term healthcare costs** *(bold)*
            - is an economic burden on → **Consumers** *(yellow)*
            - harms → **Harm to humans**

**Agricultural nutrient runoff**
- caused by → (connects to Digital Twins / precision strategies — see right side)
- prevents → (dashed line, connects to precision strategies)

---

## Digital Twins Branch (Right Side)

**Cyber Agricultural/Coastal Systems** *(teal, bold)*
- utilise → **Digital Twins** *(teal)*
- validated by ← Texas A&M AgriLife Digital Twin Research
  - ← validated by ← Wageningen University Smart Droplets Case Study

**Digital Twins**
- run →
  - Environment contam'n thresholds *(teal)*
  - History of crop growth *(teal)*
  - Per-crop nutrient/water needs *(teal)*
  - (Soil) water cycle model *(teal)*
  - (Soil) nutrient availability model *(teal)*
  - Water nutrients *(purple)*
- obtain / collect →
  - (data inputs from sensors)
- provide data to → **Cost/benefit agriculture-environment decision making** *(bold gray)*
  - consisting of →
    - **Precise fertiliser treatment** *(bold gray)*
      - leads to → **Decreased erosion** *(bold gray)*
        - increases → **Crop yield** *(bold gray)*
          - direct indicator of → Production Volume Per Labour Unit (Indicator 2.3.1) (Ref) *(pink)*
      - decreases → **Agricultural nutrient runoff**
    - **Precise irrigation strategies** *(bold gray)*
      - lead to → Better crop water supply
        - increases → **Crop yield**

**Crop yield**
- increases → **Proportion of Sustainable Agricultural Area (Indicator 2.4.1) (Ref)** *(pink)*

---

## Physical Twin Branch (Right Side)

**Agri/coastal UGV Swarms (Physical Twin)** *(purple)*
- equipped with → **Sensors** *(bold)*
  - including →
    - Thermal *(purple subsystem)*
    - Soil moisture *(purple subsystem)*
    - Plant transpiration *(purple subsystem)*
    - Soil nutrients *(purple subsystem)*
  - monitors → Soil moisture, Plant transpiration, Thermal
- including → **Precision robotic fert./sprinklers** *(purple subsystem)*
  - guides → **Plant identification camera** *(purple subsystem)*
- gather data for & execute → **Cost/benefit agriculture-environment decision making**

**Sensors**
- simulate & steer → (Soil) water cycle model, (Soil) nutrient availability model, Water nutrients, Per-crop nutrient/water needs
